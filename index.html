Our Story

It started in university. Sylvester and Ebube met as Software Engineering students at Topfaith University, and from day one, the chemistry was undeniable. What began as small freelance jobs — building apps and websites for local businesses and companies to earn some extra money as students — slowly turned into something bigger.

Project after project, we kept getting better and kept getting referred to new clients. Attendance systems powered by QR codes and facial recognition, a cafeteria management system for our own university, mobile apps, and business websites — we were building, testing, breaking, and rebuilding until things worked. Eventually, the side hustle had a name and a direction of its own.

Acies Africa Technologies was founded in 2024 with a clear mission: deliver world-class software to African businesses at prices that don't break the bank. In January 2026, we made it official with our Corporate Affairs Commission registration. From government ministries to neighbourhood clinics, from school campuses to hotel lobbies — we build solutions that work for Africa's unique landscape. And we're just getting started.
